Our goal is to explore how solar PV contributes to sustainable and affordable energy solutions, and how PV subsidies support the expansion of solar energy adoption, including among the low-income population of California.

Nurym Ayazbayev, MC/MPA

Nurym holds a MBA in International Business from Ajou University (South Korea), a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in International Economics from KazEU, and Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) from M. Narikbayev KazGUU University, Kazakhstan. Throughout his career, he has served in various capacities within the Government of Kazakhstan and multilateral financial organizations, such ADB, AIIB, and EDB. His roles have included development of investment policy, public-private partnerships, budget investments, financial sector development, economic development and trade, state budget planning, and cooperation with international financial institutions.
